Our Story

Nova Computer Academy is a government-recognized ICT training institute located in Sri Lanka. We are dedicated to providing high-quality, practical computer education that prepares our students for the modern technology-driven workplace.

Established with a vision to make quality ICT education accessible to all, our academy has grown to become one of the trusted names in vocational ICT training in the region. We are registered with the Tertiary & Vocational Education Commission (TVEC) of Sri Lanka and offer nationally recognized qualifications.(TEVET Registration No: P07/0128)

Over the years, we have trained hundreds of students who have gone on to build successful careers as ICT technicians, computer operators, network administrators, and self-employed IT entrepreneurs. Our commitment to hands-on, practical training sets us apart from traditional classroom-only approaches.

Our dedicated team of qualified instructors brings real-world industry experience into the classroom, ensuring that every student gains the skills and confidence they need to succeed in the workforce.
